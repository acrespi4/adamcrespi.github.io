January 2024 - April 2024

During my four-month internship at IRDI System, I worked as an Embedded Software Intern, focusing on firmware testing, hardware validation, and system R&D.


IRDI System - A Global Leader in Hydrogen Vehicle Refueling Technology

IRDI System specializes in developing advanced diagnostic and refueling technologies for hydrogen-powered vehicles. Their products ensure safe, efficient, and standardized hydrogen refueling by implementing SAE J2799 protocols. By integrating firmware and embedded electronics, IRDI System plays a crucial role in advancing the hydrogen fuel infrastructure for sustainable transportation.

Major Projects

  • Firmware Testing & Validation: Conducted edge-case testing on embedded firmware using JTAG Debuggers.


  • Conformal Coating Testing: Ran experiments evaluating the effectiveness of different conformal coatings for water resistance


  • Hardware Development: Built and tested an ultra-cold freezer system for component evaluation.
  • NFC Development: Worked on NDEF compatible NFC system for possible product line

Skills Developed

  • Embedded Firmware Development
  • Hardware Debugging & Validation
  • System Design
  • JTAG Debugging & Low-Level Testing

Takeaways

This experience provided me with hands-on exposure to real-world embedded systems and firmware-hardware integration. I developed strong troubleshooting skills, learned to work with cutting-edge diagnostic tools, and optimized manufacturing processes for better efficiency.

Moving forward, these skills will directly contribute to my work in robotics, Edge AI, and real-time embedded control systems.
