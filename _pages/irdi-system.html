Embedded Software Intern at IRDI System

January 2024 - April 2024

During my four-month internship at IRDI System, I worked as an Embedded Software Intern, focusing on firmware testing, hardware validation, and embedded system development. I collaborated with engineers on the diagnostic receiver product used for hydrogen vehicle refueling and was involved in both software and hardware optimization.

Key Responsibilities

  • Firmware Testing & Validation: Conducted edge-case testing on embedded firmware using JTAG Debuggers.
  • Hydrogen Fueling System Analysis: Designed test messages following the SAE J2799 standard.
  • Conformal Coating Testing: Ran experiments evaluating the effectiveness of different conformal coatings for water resistance
  • Hardware Development: Built and tested an ultra-cold freezer system for component evaluation.
  • NFC Development: Worked on NDEF compatible NFC system for possible product line
  • Manufacturing Process Optimization: Improved production by switching to laser engraving.
